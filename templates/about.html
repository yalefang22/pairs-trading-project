About Us

The Creators - Eric Kim, Yale Fang, Kyle Lee

We are students attending Bergen County Academies in the Academy for Technology and Computer Science (ATCS) as students interested in cryptocurrency as a part of our final end-of-year Capstone project.

Our Goal

This is a site designed to be your one stop shop for cryptocurrency pairs trading needs! Backtest, analyze, and graph each type of cyptocurrency here!

Tutorial

To use the application, select a cryptocurrency in each of the dropdown menus to compare. Select the program you wish to perform, as well as the dates you would like to see the data in. Submit, and view the results!

The Data

Our data comes from https://www.cryptodatadownload.com/data/gemini/, a website dedicated to giving free historical cryptocurrency data.
